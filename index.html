SERVICES


Here at Adabia Transportation, we care about you, our guest. Offering luxury accommodations is our number one priority from start to finish. Below you can find a few of those selections.



Airport Transport

Picking up or dropping off. Our guests can schedule according to their convenience, and be assured that their vehicle will be waiting for them when they land. With your busy schedules, having a reliable mode of transportation service can take yet another task off of our mind.


Hourly Service

This service is popular among our guest who travel extensively on business, or who are tending to multiple tasks around the city. Naturally, many of our guests rely on hourly service for visiting their favorite restaurants, cultural sites, entertainment venues, or sporting events.


Door to Door

With our door-to-door service, our guests have the option not only to pick up others along the way to their final destination, but customize their travel plans altogether. This takes the worry out of hidden charges. This certainly is more convenient than a straight here-to-there option that leaves no space for customization.

Reservations

When you are ready to book, it would be our pleasure to assist in setting up a reservation to go along with your travel plans. The purpose of reservations is to prepare in advance and make accommodations for each guest and their ride.



Pricings

Pricing for each trip is solely based on service, and hours.



Cancellations

Due to any unforeseen circumstances, if a cancellation is required, please do not hesitate to contact us. All cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance. There will be a 50% charge applied on last minute cancellations.
